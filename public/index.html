About Me

Erik Golden

Web Developer

I was raised in Peachtree City, Georgia, and currently live in Payson, Utah. Growing up I always loved learning about and using technology. It all began many years ago with an $800 commodore 64 computer (oh how far we've come!).


My love for learning and technology persists today, and I have spent much of my life pursuing those passions. I attended Utah Valley University where I received my Associates of Science. I am currently finishing a bachelors degree in university studies with a minor in computer science and earth science with anticipated 2018 graduation. I also attended DevMountain's full stack program to further my knowledge in web development and programming.


I look forward to working as a web developer, and continuing to learn and grow. Although that commodore 64 left me long ago, the love for technology it instilled never has.
